 AC: Alternating Current
	Periodic alternating current from positive/negative
	Example: The Mains

DC: Direct Current
	Constant amplitude from anode to cathode
	Example: Batteries

$V_{AC} = V_{pk} cos(\omega t + \phi)$
	$\omega = 2\pi f$
	$f$ is frequency in Hz

$P_{AC} = \frac{1}{T} \int_{0}^T Vi \: dt$
$P_{AC} = V_{rms}I_{rms}$
